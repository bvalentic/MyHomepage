About Me

My name is Bob Valentic. I'm a twenty six-year-old software developer living in Detroit, MI. I graduated from Michigan State University - class of 2014, go green! - with a BS in Astrophysics and a minor in Film Studies. I enjoy unwinding by going to concerts (ask me about the last five concerts I've been to!) and playing music of my own. Some other hobbies include watching movies (sci-fi and space movies are my favorite, see college major listed above), writing, and even curling up with the occasional book. Many of these hobbies usually involve a nice glass of beer while I'm doing them.
